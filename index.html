MS-Power BI

For a couple of years now, The Sports Industry is emerging diversely. There is data available in abundance and it is imperative to analyze these data. We see a huge traction in this field caused by the recent Football, Cricket and Kabaddi tournaments. And since Kabaddi is originated from and played in India, we have chosen the topic "Sport Analysis of Pro Kabaddi League using Classification Techniques" for our minor project project for this semester. One of the objectives of our project was to analyze the team performance of Top 3 teams of this league; for which we used MS-Power BI to make it more interactive. We created the dataset from scratch and made this simple dashboard. We displayed the results season wise as well as key-metrices' wise.
